About Me

I hold a PhD in Chemical Engineering and have spent the past decade as a Research Officer at the National Research Council of Canada. My work focuses on advanced chemical processes, modeling, simulation, and the development of predictive mathematical frameworks for complex systems.

My research integrates fundamental science with applied engineering, supporting innovation in materials, energy, and industrial technologies. I have contributed to peer‑reviewed publications, patents, and collaborative research initiatives across academia, government, and industry.
