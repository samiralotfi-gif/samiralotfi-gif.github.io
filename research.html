Research Areas

Chemical Process Modeling

Development of mathematical and computational models for chemical processes, including reaction kinetics, transport phenomena, and multi‑phase systems.

Research on material behavior, catalytic processes, and structure‑property relationships for industrial applications.

Creation of simulation tools and predictive frameworks to support process optimization, scale‑up, and experimental design.

Collaboration with industry partners to translate scientific insights into practical engineering solutions.
